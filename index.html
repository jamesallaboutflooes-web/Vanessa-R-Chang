My Experience

Work

2008: CAP Program, assisting the Asia Pacific team with legal compliance risks in investment projects.

2011: CAP Associate, reviewing multinational Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects submitted by all Capital Group branches in the Asia Pacific region (Hong Kong, Singapore, Tokyo). Accompanied the Los Angeles Program Office on field visits to local non-profit organizations in Los Angeles.

2013: Philanthropy & CSR Associate, responsible for the initial review of grants in a specific segment of the Greater Los Angeles area. Early exploration of high-tech philanthropy (iPad mobile teaching, online open courses).

2015: Senior Philanthropy & ESG Analyst, developing a "Social Return on Investment (SROI)" model for charitable grants to assess the specific use of charitable funds.

2018: Associate Program Officer, blockchain and early cryptocurrency philanthropy, Innovative Grant Portfolio. Negotiated with tech philanthropists in Silicon Beach and Silicon Valley, and drafted grant proposals.

2020: Philanthropic Grants Program Officer, responsible for approving charitable grants, funding cutting-edge "Web3 Philanthropy" projects, managing and reviewing the compliance of "Crypto Donations," funding technology education, and combating the "digital-Web3 divide."
